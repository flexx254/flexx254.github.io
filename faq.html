Frequently Asked Questions

When does the academic year begin?

The academic year starts in early September and ends in late June.

Is Greenwood an international curriculum school?

Yes, we follow a globally recognized international curriculum that prepares students for higher education worldwide.

What is the student-to-teacher ratio?

We maintain a low student-to-teacher ratio of approximately 15:1 to ensure personalized attention.

Do you provide school transportation?

Yes, we offer safe and reliable transport services across several routes in the city.

Are there extracurricular activities?

Absolutely! Students can join sports, music, coding, drama, and leadership clubs.
