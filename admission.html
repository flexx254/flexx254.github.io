Admissions

Join Greenwood International School

We welcome students from diverse backgrounds and nationalities. Our admissions process is simple and student-friendly. We value excellence, curiosity, and discipline in every learner.

Application Process

Here's how to apply:

  • Download and fill the application form
  • Attach previous academic transcripts
  • Submit a copy of birth certificate
  • Attach passport-sized photos (2)
  • Submit to the school or email us at admissions@greenwoodschool.ac.ke

Important Dates

Term 1 Intake: January 10, 2026

Application Deadline: December 15, 2025

Admission Requirements

  • Completed Application Form
  • Copy of Birth Certificate
  • Two Passport Photos
  • Previous School Report or Recommendation
